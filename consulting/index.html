Best Fit

When you should bring me in

You have useful data, but your current reporting process is too manual, too scattered, or too hard to explain. You need someone who can work between business questions and technical implementation.

  • Your team runs important decisions through spreadsheets or exports.
  • Dashboards exist, but people argue over which numbers are right.
  • Leadership needs clearer visibility into operations, programs, or customers.
  • You want to test an AI-assisted reporting or research workflow without overbuilding.
Fit Check

How I keep projects focused

I am most useful for focused analytics builds and early systems. The best projects have a clear business owner, a real decision to support, and enough access to understand the data.

  • No clear decision-maker or stakeholder for the reporting.
  • No willingness to define metrics or change the current workflow.
  • Pure software engineering work with no analytics or decision layer.
  • Projects that need a large agency team from day one.

Process

Small enough to move fast, structured enough to be useful.

The goal is not to impress people with tooling. The goal is to make one important decision process clearer, faster, and easier to repeat.

Every project starts by defining the business question, available data, success criteria, and stakeholder audience. From there I build the simplest useful version, review it with you, refine it, and hand it off with documentation.
