Process

Small enough to move fast, structured enough to be useful.

The goal is not to impress people with tooling. The goal is to make one important decision process clearer, faster, and easier to repeat.

Every project starts by defining the business question, available data, success criteria, and stakeholder audience. For growth work, that includes the hypothesis, KPI hierarchy, traffic or audience logic, test design, and what action the result should inform. From there I build the simplest useful version, review it with you, refine it, and hand it off with documentation.
