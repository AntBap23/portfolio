About Me

I'm a data-driven problem solver at Northern Illinois University, majoring in Operations & Information Management with a Computer Science minor. I serve as an ACM Community Ambassador and conduct AI research on LLM applications for qualitative analysis.

As a Student Consultant for Mystery Street Recording Company, I deliver strategic marketing and HR recommendations for a Chicago-based audio studio. I also research LLM applications, developing custom GPT frameworks and NLP pipelines that analyze AI and human-generated text.

Previously, I designed Tableau systems for delivery performance at Shipt and built automated dashboards at Innovation DuPage. I'm passionate about applying data-driven solutions to solve complex business challenges.
