How much does an analytics dashboard project cost?

A focused analytics dashboard project with Anthony Baptiste typically falls in the $3,500-$10,000 range, depending on the number of data sources, dashboard complexity, cleanup work, and handoff needs. Smaller Analytics Clarity Audits start at $750 when a team needs direction before a full dashboard engagement.

What's the difference between an analytics audit and a dashboard build?

An Analytics Clarity Audit reviews KPIs, dashboards, data quality, and reporting workflows to identify what is broken or unclear. A Dashboard and Reporting Sprint turns that direction into a usable reporting asset with cleaned data, dashboard views, metric definitions, and handoff notes.

Can you analyze Adobe Target / A/B test results?

I can support Adobe Target, AB Tasty, Adobe Analytics, Customer Journey Analytics, A/B testing, and multivariate testing analysis. The work focuses on test setup checks, audience and traffic quality, KPI impact, segmentation, and clear next-step recommendations.

Do you work with startups and nonprofits?

Yes. I am a good fit for startups, nonprofits, university research teams, and small businesses that have useful data but need clearer metrics, reporting workflows, or decision support before they scale the process.
