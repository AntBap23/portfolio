About Me

I'm an Operations & Information Management major with a Computer Science minor at Northern Illinois University, and my work has mostly focused on helping teams use data more clearly and confidently. I'm especially drawn to problems that sit between analytics, reporting, and business decision-making.

My experience includes operational analytics at Shipt, reporting and dashboard work at Innovation DuPage, consulting through NIU's Experiential Learning Center, and research at the NIU College of Business on how AI-generated personas and simulated interviews can support qualitative research design. Across those experiences, I’ve spent a lot of time turning messy information into dashboards, frameworks, and insights that people can actually use.

I enjoy the mix of technical and business thinking that comes with analytics work: understanding the problem, structuring the data, building something reliable, and communicating it in a way that makes sense to stakeholders. That’s the kind of work I want to keep doing in business intelligence, analytics engineering, data engineering, and related roles.
