Positioning

Analytics consulting grounded in experimentation, operations, growth, and research

I am an Operations & Information Management major with a Computer Science minor at Northern Illinois University. My background combines BI, analytics engineering, digital experimentation at Ulta Beauty, Adobe Target and AB Tasty test analysis, operational reporting, supply chain analytics, and applied AI research.

That mix matters for consulting because most teams do not just need a dashboard. They need someone who can understand the decision, clean up the data path, define the metrics, validate the experiment, build the reporting, and explain what changed in plain business language.

I am especially strong with teams that have useful data trapped in spreadsheets, Salesforce, web analytics exports, ecommerce tests, operations systems, research transcripts, or one-off reporting processes. My job is to turn that into something repeatable, credible, and easier to act on.
