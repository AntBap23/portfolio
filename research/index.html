Research

Applied AI research focused on qualitative interview design.

My research explores how AI-generated personas and LLM-based interview simulations can strengthen interview-based qualitative research. Rather than treating AI as a replacement for human participants or researchers, the work focuses on using it as a methodological aid for refining interview protocols, surfacing researcher assumptions, and stress-testing early interpretations before larger conclusions are formed.

Research Paper

This work examines how matched AI and human interviews can be compared to understand where LLMs are helpful, where they fall short, and how they can be used responsibly in qualitative research workflows. A central theme of the project is augmentation rather than automation: using AI to make researchers sharper without replacing human judgment.

Qualitative AI Studio

This separate research tool extends the project into a more interactive workflow, showing how AI can support persona-based interview simulation, transcript comparison, and qualitative interpretation in a more guided environment. It was built to make the research process easier to explore, demonstrate, and apply beyond a static presentation format.
